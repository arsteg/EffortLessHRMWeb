Time Tracking Features

1.Attendance Monitoring

Simplify attendance tracking with EffortlessHRM Time Tracker. Easily monitor employee start and end times in a structured format, eliminating the need for timecards and excessive paperwork.

2.Automatic Time Tracking

Gain insights into your team's productivity with detailed analytics on work time. EffortlessHRM automatically tracks time, providing timesheet reports and analytics to identify time-wasting activities and optimize productivity.

3.Real-Time Reports

Access accurate data on tracked hours, productivity levels, and computer usage with timely reports presented in clear charts and tables for easy analysis.

Measure employee performance based on keystrokes and mouse clicks, allowing you to gauge productivity levels effectively.
